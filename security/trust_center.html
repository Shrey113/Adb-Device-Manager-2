We believe utility software should be secure by design. ADB Device Manager operates entirely locally, keeping your sensitive communications, credentials, and screen streams isolated on your hardware. We do not operate cloud servers, track user sessions, or monetise data.

Use the resources below to understand our architecture, privacy policies, and how you can audit our practices.

Security & Privacy Resources
