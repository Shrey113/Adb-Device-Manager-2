# Audio Cast Copy

Stream your Android device's audio directly to your Windows PC or Web browser with near zero latency.

## Overview

Audio Cast Copy creates a real-time copy of your Android device's internal audio and transmits it instantly to a receiver. This is perfect for gaming, watching videos, or monitoring audio apps on a larger system without delay.

## Key Features

- **Low Latency Mode**: Optimized for near real-time audio synchronization.
- **Multiple Receivers**: Send audio to a native Windows receiver application or a Web-based receiver.
- **High Quality**: Captures raw audio for clear sound reproduction.
- **Seamless Integration**: Works alongside screen mirroring or as a standalone audio stream.

## How to Use

1. **Install App**: Download and install the app on your Android device from [here](https://shrey113.github.io/Adb-Device-Manager-2/android_download.html).
2. **Connect**: Connect your Android device to your Windows PC (via USB or Wi-Fi).
3. **Start Audio Stream**: In the App Mode menu, select "Audio Cast Copy".
4. **Choose Receiver**:
   - **Open in Windows**: Stream audio directly to the native Windows application.
   - **Open Web URL**: Generate a local link to listen via any web browser.

## Technical Details

This feature utilizes advanced audio capture APIs on Android to replicate the audio stream and efficient network protocols to ensure minimal delay (latency), providing an immersive experience whether you are gaming or streaming media.
