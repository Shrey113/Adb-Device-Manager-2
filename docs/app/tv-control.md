# TV Control

Control your Android TV or Google TV devices directly from your local network.

## Overview

The TV Control feature allows you to turn your device into a powerful remote for your Android TV. Easily discover devices on your local Wi-Fi, pair with them, and navigate your TV interface with ease.

## Key Features

- **Smart Remote**: Full remote control capabilities including navigation, volume, and home buttons.
- **Local Network Discovery**: Automatically finds compatible Android TV devices on the same network.
- **Easy Pairing**: Simple pairing process to securely connect your controller to the TV.
- **TV Cast**: Cast photos and videos from your device directly to the big screen.

## How to Use

1. **Install App**: Download and install the app on your Android device from [here](https://shrey113.github.io/Adb-Device-Manager-2/android_download.html).
2. **Find Device**: Open the "TV Control" section in the app and scan for Android TV devices on your local network.
3. **Pair Remote**: Select your TV from the list and follow the pairing instructions (usually entering a code displayed on TV).
4. **Control & Cast**: Use the digital remote to navigate your TV or select media to cast photos and videos.
